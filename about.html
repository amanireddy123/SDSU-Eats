About Us

Welcome to SDSU Eats. Designed by the students of SDSU for SDSU. This website is solely designed for educational purposes. Anish, Ajender, Amani, Shivani and Sumedh are the contributors of this project.

Order food with ease!

This project was designed for EE561: Database and Web Development taught by Professor Ugur Dogan. This is an interactive website that can make it easier for college students to order food on campus. Right now, students often have to wait in long lines to order food, but with SDSUEats, they can order from any campus food stall using a single website. This means they won't have to wait in line, and all the food stalls on campus will be available on the website. It's like a one-stop-shop for ordering food without the hassle of waiting in line.
